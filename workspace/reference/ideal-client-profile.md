# Ideal Client Profile

## Primary: GIG Segment
- Company size: 50-249 FTEs
- Revenue: $10M-$50M
- Pain: Founders doing too much, managers promoted without training
- Entry point: EEA or Lead Better

## Secondary: CCS Segment
- Company size: 250-599 FTEs
- Revenue: $50M-$150M
- Pain: Leadership team misalignment, inconsistent management quality
- Entry point: ELT-Dev or MMP-AI

## Sweet Spot
- 100-300 employees, 10-30 managers
- Growing fast enough that management gaps are visible
- Founder is coachable and values systems thinking

## Disqualifiers
- Under 50 employees (too early)
- Over 600 employees (enterprise sales cycle, not our model)
- No budget for development (looking for free resources)
