# **Section 3: Give-First Engagement Sequence**

The Frank Kern philosophy applied to GIG/CCS community engagement: contribute expertise first, build visible authority, then earn the right to a business conversation.

**MONTH 1-2: Listen & Contribute**

Join Rands \+ CTO Craft \+ E-MGMT Slack. Lurk for 2 weeks, then answer 3-5 questions per week using your management effectiveness expertise. No links, no pitches — just value. Post on r/ExperiencedDevs when you see management pain threads. Comment on podcast episodes on LinkedIn.

**MONTH 3-4: Deepen & Connect**

Apply to alphalist \+ CTO Connection. Begin DMs in CTO Craft with people you’ve helped publicly. Attend a LeadDev meetup or 7CTOs chapter event. Pitch 2-3 podcasts (start with Tech Lead Journal and CTO Think — most approachable).

**MONTH 5-6: Activate & Convert**

Pitch ELC Podcast and Modern CTO. Explore 7CTOs partner program. Pitch a LeadingEng roundtable or LeadDev talk. By now your community contributions have built the trust that makes outbound warm instead of cold.
