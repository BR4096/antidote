# **Section 4 — CleanShot Personalized Video**

The highest-leverage single addition in v2.1. Replaces the plain text DM at Touch 2 with a 90-second personalized video. Research on personalized video outreach shows 3–5× higher reply rates when at least one piece of highly specific information is included — the direct report name-drop is that element.

## **Video Structure (90 seconds — do not exceed)**

| TIME | SECTION | WHAT TO SAY |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| 0–15s | The hook | "Hey \[First Name\] — saw your post about \[exact post topic\]. That's the exact problem I work on." |
| 15–45s | The insight | "Companies at \[Company\]'s stage — you've built a team of about \[size\], raised \[round\] — almost always hit the same wall: decision velocity drops 30–40% not because people got worse but because the leadership infrastructure didn't scale. I've seen it at \[Name\] and \[Name\]'s level especially." (name direct reports here — the expertise frame) |
| 45–65s | The bridge | "I built an 8-minute diagnostic that shows exactly which of 6 dimensions is creating that friction for you specifically — benchmarked against other Series A/B companies your size. Zero commitment, instant results." |
| 65–80s | The CTA | "Link is below. And if the results resonate — I'm opening a founding cohort on March 16, 8 of your senior team, $4,500. Happy to walk you through it. — BR" |
| 80–90s | The close | Show LinkedIn profile on screen. Smile. End. |

## **Background Setup in CleanShot**

* Open the prospect's LinkedIn profile in Chrome, scroll to show their name, headline, and company — the 'above the fold' view that every viewer will immediately recognize as their own profile

* Set CleanShot window capture to the browser window — the viewer sees themselves before they hear a word

* Use your webcam overlay in the bottom-right corner at 25% size — face visible, profile dominant

* Lighting: face well-lit, no backlight. 90 seconds at low production quality beats polished at 3 minutes.

## **The Direct Reports Name-Drop — Framing Rules**

This element has the highest signal value AND the highest misfire risk. The difference is the frame:

| WRONG FRAME (surveillance) | RIGHT FRAME (expertise) |
| :---- | :---- |
| "I know \[Name\] and \[Name\] are your direct reports..." — this is just reciting information. It reads as surveillance. | "I've seen it at \[Name\] and \[Name\]'s level especially — that coordination gap between the founder and the first VP layer is exactly where the friction lives at your stage." — this uses their names as evidence of expertise. |
